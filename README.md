# travel-agent
Personal travel agent that can: <br>
1. Suggest cities to travel to in consideration of 1. safety 2. affordability 3. ease of travel 4. fun & interesting places or activities
   - Provide itinerary
   - Food places
3. Provide weather info and what to wear
4. Flight info
5. Hotel/Airbnb info
6. Ground its suggestions based on latest news surrounding the city
